Cricket Game Rules

Cricket is a game played with a bat and ball on a large field, known as a ground, between two teams of 11 players each. The object of the game is to score runs when at bat and to put out, or dismiss, the opposing batsmen when in the field. The cricket rules displayed on this page here are for the traditional form of cricket which is called "Test Cricket",One Day International 50 over matches, Twenty20 Cricket etc where the rules differ slightly.
