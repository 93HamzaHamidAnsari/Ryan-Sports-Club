Football Game Rules

The game can be played on either natural or artificial surfaces, the surface must be green and rectangular in shape. The two long sides of the rectangle are called touch lines and the two shorter sides are called goal lines. The field is divided in half by the halfway line. Two teams of no more than 11 players (one of which is the goalkeeper). A game cannot start if either team has less than 7 players.

The rules of football are officially referred to as the "Laws of the Game". The Laws of the Game are described very precisely (approximately 150 pages) on the FIFA website. There are 17 laws in total, each one briefly summarised below in a word file. .
